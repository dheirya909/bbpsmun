Committees

UNGA-DISEC

Agenda: Addressing Weaponisation of AI and Ensuring Ethical Use of Emerging Technologies in Warfare

The committee witnessed engaging debates on accountability in AI-enabled weapon systems, preventing misuse of autonomous technologies, and promoting frameworks for responsible innovation in defense sectors.

UNHRC

Agenda: Protecting Human Rights and Accountability of States for Human Rights Abuses Against Minority & Indigenous Communities

Passionate discussions addressed systemic discrimination, marginalization, and state-led violations with solutions rooted in justice, equality, and international cooperation.

LOK SABHA

Agenda: Formulation and Implementation of a Uniform Civil Code with Constitutional Compliance

Dynamic discussions on balancing uniformity in personal laws with respect for religious and cultural diversity, emphasizing gender equality and minority rights.

INTERNATIONAL PRESS

Documenting proceedings, conducting interviews, and producing press releases with professionalism and creativity

Journalists, photographers, and caricaturists captured the spirit of debate and diplomacy throughout the conference.
